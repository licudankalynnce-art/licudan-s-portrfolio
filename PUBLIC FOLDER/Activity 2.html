LIANNE's Chicken? à la Hala Sige Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (any cut)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup toyo
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 tbsp calamansi
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 small onion, sliced

Kitchen Tools Needed:

  • CHOPPING BOARD – for cutting the chicken
  • KNIFE – to slice the chicken, onion and calamansi
  • FRYING PAN – for cooking everything
  • COOKING SPOON/ SPATULA – for stirring and flipping
  • MEASURING SPOON – for toyo, vinegar, sugar, and oil
  • MEASURING CUP – for the water
  • SMALL BOWL – for the chicken container
  • STOVE – heat source
  • PLATE – for serving

Procedures:

  1. Cut the chicken, onion, and calamansi into serving pieces and set aside.
  2. Make fire in the stove. And get your frying pan and put it in the stove, then wait until it's hot.
  3. Heat oil in the pan and sauté the onion then lightly brown the chicken until the edges look toasted.
  4. Mix toyo, vinegar, calamansi, sugar, and salt, then pour over the chicken. (Mix well the ingredients)
  5. Add water, cover, and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Remove the cover and cook until the sauce turns sticky and slightly burnt-sweet.
  7. Turn off heat and let it rest for 2 minutes before serving.
